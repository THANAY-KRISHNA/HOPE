About IEDC

Our Mission

To foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students, providing them with the resources, mentorship, and support needed to transform creative ideas into successful ventures that make a difference in society.

Our Vision

To become a leading hub for innovation and entrepreneurship, producing successful startups and entrepreneurs who make a significant impact on society and the economy through groundbreaking solutions.

Our Programs

Startup Incubation

Comprehensive support for early-stage startups including mentorship, funding opportunities, workspace, and technical resources to accelerate growth.

Workshops & Training

Regular workshops on entrepreneurship, innovation, and skill development conducted by industry experts and successful entrepreneurs.

Competitions

Organize and participate in national and international entrepreneurship competitions, hackathons, and pitch events to showcase talent.
