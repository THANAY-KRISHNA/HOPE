Welcome to ZENORIX 2025

ZENORIX 2025 is a premier technical festival organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) at IES College of Engineering, Chittilappilly.

This two-day extravaganza brings together the brightest minds in technology for competitions, workshops, and interactive experiences.

Join us on September 26th & 27th, 2025, for an unforgettable journey into the world of innovation, creativity, and technological excellence.
