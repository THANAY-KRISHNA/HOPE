About ECE TechFest

Celebrating Innovation in Electrical & Communication Engineering

ECE TechFest is the annual technical festival organized by the Department of Electrical and Communication Engineering. It serves as a platform for students, professionals, and enthusiasts to showcase their skills, knowledge, and innovations in the field of electrical and communication engineering.

The festival features a wide range of events including technical competitions, workshops, seminars, and exhibitions that cover various aspects of ECE such as signal processing, telecommunications, VLSI design, embedded systems, and emerging technologies.
