Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior programming experience?

No. If you’ve ever typed a question into ChatGPT, you already have what it takes. We start from zero. No code. No jargon. Just clear steps to make AI do what you want — even if you’ve never written a single line.

What tools/software do I need?

Your laptop or phone. That’s it. Everything runs in your browser — no downloads, no accounts, no setup. We’ll give you instant access to the exact tools we use live during the session.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Everyone who attends gets a certificate — signed, dated, and ready to add to LinkedIn or your resume. It doesn’t say “attended.” It says “built an AI workflow.” That’s the difference.

Is there any post-workshop support?

You’ll get all the prompts, templates, and guides we use — instantly. After the session, you’re not left alone. You’ll be invited into the same group where students are already using these tools to automate assignments, build projects, and win challenges.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refund if you cancel 7 days before. After that, you can transfer your seat to someone else — no questions asked. This isn’t a locked-in commitment. It’s a chance to show up and see if this changes how you work.

How is this workshop different from other AI courses?

Most AI courses teach you how AI works. This one teaches you how to control it. You won’t learn theory. You’ll learn how to make AI summarize research, write reports, or explain concepts — by typing the right words. No coding. Just results.

How long is the workshop?

105 minutes. From 9:15 AM to 10:45 AM. You’ll walk in as a user. You’ll walk out with a working AI tool you built yourself.

Is there homework or assignments?

No. But if you want to go further, we’ll give you optional prompts to try at home — like turning your notes into flashcards or automating email drafts. Do it if you want. Skip it if you don’t. The core skill? Learned in the session.

Are sessions recorded?

No. Because the magic happens when you’re doing it live — asking questions, seeing AI react, fixing your prompt on the spot. You’ll get everything we use afterward, but the real value? Only here, in the room, with the clock ticking.

Can students attend?

This is made for students. Not engineers. Not researchers. Students. Whether you're in CSE, ECE, or Civil Engineering — if you’ve ever wasted hours writing a report or summarizing notes, this is for you.

Will I work on real projects?

You’ll build one — during the session. A real AI assistant that can summarize articles, generate quizzes, or draft emails. Not a demo. Not a simulation. Something you can open tomorrow morning and use to save time on your next assignment.

What if I’m in a different time zone?

This is happening at IES College of Engineering, Thrissur — as part of Techfest. It’s a live, in-person event. If you can’t make it, you miss the only chance to build something with us. Materials will be shared later — but the moment you become the person who knows how to make AI work for them? That only happens if you show up.
