WARNING: HIGH CURRENT

The robotic servos operate on high-amperage external power. Improper wiring may result in component failure or thermal events.

01 // Eye Protection

Safety glasses must be worn at all times during soldering sessions and when the robotic arm is in "Active Torque" mode. Mechanical failure can result in high-velocity debris.

02 // LiPo Battery Handling

Lithium Polymer batteries are volatile. Do not puncture, short circuit, or leave charging unattended. All batteries must be stored in the provided fire-safe bags when not in use.

03 // Emergency Stop

Familiarize yourself with the hardware kill-switch location on your workbench. In the event of a code loop causing erratic movement, cut power immediately.
