About ZENORIX 2025

Experience the future of technology at IES College of Engineering

Welcome to ZENORIX 2025

ZENORIX 2025 is a premier technical festival organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) at IES College of Engineering, Chittilappilly. This two-day extravaganza brings together the brightest minds in technology for competitions, workshops, and interactive experiences.

Join us on September 26th & 27th, 2025, for an unforgettable journey into the world of innovation, creativity, and technological excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ZENORIX 2025

ZENORIX 2025 is a two-day technical festival organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) at IES College of Engineering, featuring competitions, workshops, and fun activities.

You can register for events by clicking the "Register via Google Form" button in the Registration section. Fill out the form with your details and select the events you want to participate in.

Registration fees vary by event. Some events are free to attend, while others may have a nominal fee. Please check the event details or contact the organizers for specific fee information.

Bring a valid ID card, registration confirmation, laptop for technical events, and comfortable clothing. Specific events may require additional materials - check event descriptions for details.

Food and refreshments will be available at the venue. Accommodation is not provided, but we can assist you in finding nearby hotels if needed.

You can contact the organizers through the contact form on our website or call directly: Student Coordinator - 8089913951, Faculty Coordinator - 9446090144.
